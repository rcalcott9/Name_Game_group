Instructions Comparison

2-Player Game

Game Overview

You will work with the same partner for all 14 rounds. Each round, you both choose a letter. You only earn points when you both choose the same letter.

KEY RULE: Both players must choose the same letter to earn points

Letter Choices

In each round, you and your partner will each choose one letter from options like these (there are additional letters in the actual game). Remember: you only earn points when you both choose the same letter.

J
P
R
C
D

Game Structure

Game Length

14 Rounds
Approximately 15 minutes

How It Works

  1. You are matched with one other participant
  2. You both choose your letters each round (you earn points only when you choose the same letter)
  3. You see the results showing both choices
  4. Continue to the next round

Important Notes

⚠️ Activity Requirement

If you or your partner become inactive for more than 2 minutes during the game, both of you will be redirected to complete demographic questions and receive your completion code.

⏱️ Matching Timeout

If you cannot be matched with other participants within 10 minutes, you will be redirected to complete the demographic questions and receive your completion code.

4-Player Game

Game Overview

You will be in a group of 4 players, but you work in pairs. Each round, you are paired with one other player, while the other two players form their own pair. You only earn points when all 4 players in your group choose the same letter.

KEY RULE: All 4 players must choose the same letter to earn points

Letter Choices

In each round, all 4 players in your group will each choose one letter from options like these (there are additional letters in the actual game). Remember: you only earn points when all 4 players choose the same letter.

J
P
R
C
D

Partnership System

You will be in a group of 4 players split into pairs. All 4 players must choose the same letter for anyone to earn points:

You

Choose your letter

+

Your Partner

Chooses their letter

After each round, the pairs are reshuffled so you get a new partner from your group.

Game Structure

Game Length

14 Rounds
Approximately 15 minutes

How It Works

  1. You are paired with someone from your group of 4
  2. All 4 players in your group choose their letters each round (you earn points only when all 4 choose the same letter)
  3. You see the results showing both choices
  4. Pairs are reshuffled and you get a new partner for the next round

Important Notes

⚠️ Activity Requirement

If you or your partner become inactive for more than 2 minutes during the game, both of you will be redirected to complete demographic questions and receive your completion code.

⏱️ Matching Timeout

If you cannot be matched with other participants within 10 minutes, you will be redirected to complete the demographic questions and receive your completion code.
