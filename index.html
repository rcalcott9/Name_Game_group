About This Study

This experiment examined coordination behavior in a letter-choice game with two conditions:

  • Dyadic Condition: Pairs of 2 players work together throughout all 14 rounds
  • Group Condition: Groups of 6 players must coordinate (all 6 must choose the same letter)

Key Design: In rounds 1-7, only letter J was worth points (10 pts). In rounds 8-14, letters M and N were introduced, each worth 15 points.
