Letter Coordination Game Instructions

Game Overview

{% if condition == 'dyadic' %}

You will work with the same partner for all 14 rounds. Each round, you both choose a letter. You earn points when you both choose the same letter.

{% else %}

You will be in a group of 6 players. Each round, all 6 players choose a letter. You earn points when all 6 players choose the same letter. You will see everyone's choices after each round.

{% endif %}

Letter Choices & Payment

{% if condition == 'dyadic' %}

In each round, you and your partner will each choose one letter from the available options. Each point you earn will give you a bonus of one cent (100 points gives you 1 dollar bonus).

{% else %}

In each round, all 6 players will each choose one letter from the available options. You only get the points corresponding to the letter if ALL players choose the same letter. Each point you earn will give you a bonus of one cent (100 points gives you 1 dollar bonus).

{% endif %}

Game Structure

Game Length

14 Rounds
Approximately 15 minutes

How It Works

{% if condition == 'dyadic' %}
  1. You are matched with one other participant
  2. You both choose your letters each round
  3. You earn points when you both choose the same letter
  4. You see the results showing both choices
  5. Continue to the next round with the same partner
{% else %}
  1. You are placed in a group of 6 players
  2. All 6 players choose their letters simultaneously each round
  3. You see the results showing ALL 6 players' choices
  4. You earn points ONLY if all 6 players chose the same letter
  5. Continue to the next round with the same group
  6. Use the information from previous rounds to coordinate better
{% endif %}

Important Notes

⚠️ Activity Requirement

If you or your partner become inactive for more than 2 minutes during the game, both of you will be redirected to complete demographic questions and receive your completion code.

⏱️ Matching Timeout

If you cannot be matched with other participants within 10 minutes, you will be redirected to complete the demographic questions and receive your completion code.
